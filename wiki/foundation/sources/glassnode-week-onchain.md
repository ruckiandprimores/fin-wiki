## 3-Axis Tags

- **Axis 1 — Economic mechanism**: behavioral exploitation + microstructure/on-chain flow (cohort cost-basis as support/resistance; smart-money accumulation footprints).
- **Axis 2 — Driver**: information asymmetry — on-chain visibility is *legal and public* in crypto (the "insider flow tracking" that is illegal in equities).
- **Axis 3 — Crypto applicability**: **direct, crypto-native** — no cross-market translation needed; these primitives have no TradFi equivalent.

## Key metrics (the observables)

- **Realized Price** — realized cap ÷ circulating supply ≈ the aggregate **on-chain cost basis** per coin. Acts as a structural support/resistance band.
- **True Market Mean Price** — a cointime/entity-adjusted "true" average acquisition price; paired with Realized Price it brackets a primary valuation corridor (e.g. a contemporaneous read framed "$79k True Market Mean and $54.9k Realized Price define the resistance/support corridor").
- **Short-Term-Holder MVRV (STH-MVRV)** — price ÷ short-term-holder (<155-day) cost basis; **1.0 = breakeven**, a watched flip line between support and resistance for the marginal/recent buyer cohort.
- **Accumulation Trend Score** (0–1) — whether large entities are net-accumulating; a smart-money footprint.
- Adjacent toolkit: SOPR, Coin Days Destroyed (CDD), HODL Waves, supply-in-profit/loss crossover (already referenced in [[../macro/btc-regime-taxonomy-2020-2025#regime-15-june-markers|Regime 15]]).

## Why it matters / honest scope

These are **named, thresholded observables** that fit the wiki's Hypothesis / Observable / Falsifier format — e.g. "STH-MVRV reclaims 1.0 → recent-buyer cohort back in profit" is testable. But the newsletter's outputs are **descriptive valuation/positioning reads, not pre-registered statistical edges**; the per-week directional narrative is opinion, not signal. Ingest discipline: extract the metric + threshold, not the call. **Live recurring feed** — re-pull current values at use; do not freeze a single issue. Strong candidate for the wiki's recurring-ingest discipline (alongside Investopedia + Hayes), bi-weekly cadence.
