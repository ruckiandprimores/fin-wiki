## Falsifier / mechanism thresholds

- **ETF-flow structural-bid mechanic**: a run of IBIT net-inflow days (≥1–2) functions as the
  short-horizon signal that an outflow episode has turned — the flow-based counterpart to a
  price-based read.
- **L1 structural falsifier**: a **90-day** ETF net-outflow streak is the "wounded, not fired"
  threshold tracked on the BTC card — the length of streak that would indicate a structural (not
  episodic) bid loss.
- **COIN**: the fwd P/E metric is unreliable in loss-making quarters (a GAAP-loss denominator
  collapse inflates the multiple); fwd P/S entering the 3–5× range alongside EPS stabilization is
  the more meaningful re-rating signal.
