The sweep confirmed hard that cap-weighting *is* NVDA-concentration — documenting it once prevents
re-deciding it each session and pins VVSM as the verified role-(c) overlay. The wrap-quality gate
(the "right names, wrong weights" failure mode in the [[foundation/methodology/execution-vehicle-typology|typology]]) is exactly what kills these as cores.
