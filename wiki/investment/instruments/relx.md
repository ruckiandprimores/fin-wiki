# RELX — RELX plc

> **TL;DR**: The **most constructive clean entry** of the [[investment/theses/data-moat-ai-disruption|data-moat thesis]]
> (rank-2, STRONG BUY). The Feb-3 2026 AI-legal-tool launch took RELX **−14% (steepest since 1988)** —
> but the attacked segment (Legal) is a *minority* of a diversified base (Risk / STM-Elsevier /
> Exhibitions). The drop was **legal-headline optics on an AI-immune-weighted business** → the cleanest
> Mr.-Market setup in the complex, at the **cheapest multiple vs its own history.** Dated analysis, not
> advice; verify before acting.

## Identity

- **Ticker / listing**: RELX (LSE primary; RELX ADR on NYSE; Amsterdam)
- **What it is**: global analytics + information-services group. Segments: **Risk** (largest —
  proprietary contributory data, fraud/identity/insurance analytics), **STM** (Elsevier — the
  irreplaceable scientific archive + ScienceDirect/Scopus), **Legal** (LexisNexis — the AI-*attacked*
  segment), **Exhibitions**.
- **Note**: RELX price data was flagged **noisy** in the source sweep (~$31–33 vs $34.46) — confirm the
  live quote/line before sizing.

## Role

- **Vertical**: AI/Tech data-moat.
- **Execution-vehicle role**: **direct name**. Per the
  [[foundation/methodology/execution-vehicle-typology|typology]], a direct position, not a wrapper.

## Thesis (durable)

**Diversification is the moat-protector.** The generative-AI threat is concentrated on the legal
*workflow* layer (LexisNexis vs Anthropic Claude Cowork / Harvey) — but Legal is a minority segment.
The majority sits in **Risk** (proprietary contributory data — a network of contributed records no AI
can synthesize) and **Elsevier** (an irreplaceable peer-reviewed archive that AI is a *consumer* of,
not a substitute for). So a single legal-product launch repricing the *whole* RELX is the textbook
[[foundation/market-wisdom/mr-market|Mr. Market]] overreaction — the de-rate hit the diversified base
for a minority-segment headline. Full attack/defense reasoning:
[[investment/theses/data-moat-ai-disruption|the thesis]].
