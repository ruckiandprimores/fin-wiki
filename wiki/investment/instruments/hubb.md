## Thesis (durable)

**Sell shovels, not the gold** — the AI build-out's binding constraint is **power and grid**, and
Hubbell sells the electrical hardware that build-out consumes (utility T&D + datacenter electrical).
The same generative-AI force that *threatens* the data-moats is a clean *tailwind* here. Within the
shovels group, HUBB is the **valuation-coherent entry**: it captures the grid/datacenter demand without
the ATH multiple premium VRT/ETN carry, and the $3B NSI deal extends the utility franchise.
