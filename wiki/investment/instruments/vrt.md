## Identity

- **Ticker / listing**: VRT (NYSE)
- **What it is**: critical digital-infrastructure hardware — datacenter **power** (UPS, switchgear,
  busbar) and **thermal management** (liquid cooling, the AI-density enabler). The arms-dealer to
  hyperscaler + colo datacenter build-out, with liquid cooling the AI-specific tailwind.

## Role

- **Vertical**: AI/Tech **picks-and-shovels** (physical infra).
- **Execution-vehicle role**: **direct name**, per the [[foundation/methodology/execution-vehicle-typology|typology]].

## Thesis (durable)

**The purest listed bet on datacenter physical build-out.** AI compute density is bottlenecked by power
delivery and heat removal — exactly Vertiv's product. The **~$15B backlog** converts the build-out
narrative into contracted revenue visibility, and liquid cooling is a step-change in per-rack content as
GPU density rises. Highest fundamental conviction in the shovels group; the entire reservation is
**valuation** — at ~47× the market already prices years of the build-out, so the margin-of-safety
depends on the backlog continuing to compound rather than the multiple re-rating further.

## Falsifier / invalidation

- **Backlog growth stalls or cancellations appear** — the visibility that justifies the multiple
  evaporates.
