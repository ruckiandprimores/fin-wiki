# VRT — Vertiv

> **TL;DR**: The purest listed bet on datacenter **physical build-out** — power delivery (UPS,
> switchgear, busbar) and thermal management (liquid cooling) for AI-density compute. The load-bearing
> analytical point: the **~$15B AI backlog is what converts a build-out narrative into contracted
> revenue visibility**, and the forward multiple has re-rated to reflect exactly that visibility — so
> the read is a **backlog-vs-multiple pairing**: the multiple only holds up if the backlog keeps
> compounding, and a hyperscaler capex guide-down is the fastest way both legs turn at once. *Analysis
> of mechanism and risk — not a recommendation.*

## Identity

- **Ticker / listing**: VRT (NYSE)
- **What it is**: critical digital-infrastructure hardware — datacenter **power** (UPS, switchgear,
  busbar) and **thermal management** (liquid cooling, the AI-density enabler). The arms-dealer to
  hyperscaler + colo datacenter build-out, with liquid cooling the AI-specific tailwind.

## Role

- **Vertical**: AI/Tech **picks-and-shovels** (physical infra).
- **Execution-vehicle role**: **direct name**, per the [[foundation/methodology/execution-vehicle-typology|typology]].

## Thesis (durable)

**The purest listed bet on datacenter physical build-out.** AI compute density is bottlenecked by power
delivery and heat removal — exactly Vertiv's product. The **~$15B backlog** converts the build-out
narrative into contracted revenue visibility, and liquid cooling is a step-change in per-rack content as
GPU density rises. Within the shovels group, Vertiv carries the clearest quantitative backlog signal;
the counterweight is **valuation** — at ~47× the market already prices years of the build-out, so the
margin-of-safety depends on the backlog continuing to compound rather than the multiple re-rating
further.

## Falsifier / invalidation

- **Backlog growth stalls or cancellations appear** — the visibility that justifies the multiple
  evaporates.
- **Hyperscaler capex guides down** — the demand engine for the whole shovels basket turns.

## Risks

- **Valuation / multiple compression** — at ~47× a growth wobble de-rates hard; the dominant risk.
- **Cyclicality + concentration** — datacenter capex is lumpy and hyperscaler-concentrated.
- **Competition in cooling** — liquid-cooling share is contestable as the category scales.
