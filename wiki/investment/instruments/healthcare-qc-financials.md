# Healthcare · Quality-Compounder · Financials Analyses

> **TL;DR**: The analytical distinction this page carries is between a **wide-moat de-rate** — a
> durable-franchise business trading below its own historical multiple for a reason unrelated to
> the moat (SPGI: FX-driven multiple compression, not a Market Intelligence impairment) — and a
> **falling knife** — a business trading down on a genuine deterioration in the operating story
> (BSX: product recall + guidance cut layered under a growth story). Confusing the two is the main
> failure mode this page is built to prevent. Durable mechanism + dated snapshot; no trigger is
> read live off these numbers without a fresh re-pull. *Analysis, not advice.*
