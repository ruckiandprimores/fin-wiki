## Thesis (durable)

**Electrification + AI build-out on a diversified base.** Eaton rides the same datacenter-power tailwind
as Vertiv/Hubbell but spreads it across utility grid spend, industrial electrification, and aerospace —
so the AI-datacenter torque is real but **diluted by a steadier, more cyclical-balanced** revenue mix.
That diversification is the feature: lower single-end-market risk than VRT, at the cost of less pure
AI-datacenter leverage. Record Q1 confirms the demand; the reservation is the same as the rest of the
basket — **price**, at a 52-wk-high ~30–32× multiple.

## Falsifier / invalidation

- **Hyperscaler capex guides down** AND **industrial/utility capex rolls over** — the diversified base
  loses both legs at once.

## Risks

- **Valuation** — a 52-wk-high ~31× leaves little margin of safety; multiple compression on any wobble.
- **Diversification cuts both ways** — less AI-datacenter torque than VRT if that's the leg that compounds.
