# Ex-US / Global ETF + Gold ETC Dossiers

> **TL;DR**: The two diversification / hard-money legs the sweep surfaced as gaps (the book is
> 57–71% US, no gold sleeve). Equity wrappers are [[foundation/methodology/execution-vehicle-typology|role-(a)]];
> gold ETC is **role-(b)** direct-asset-access. No single "ex-US all-world" UCITS at scale → clean
> ex-US build = **XUSE (DM ex-US) + EMIM (EM).** Gold default = **SGLN / SGLD (0.12%).** Framed as
> concentration-reduction + de-dollarization hedge, **not** an "ex-US wins" call — full case +
> steelman bear in [[investment/theses/ex-us-de-dollarization|the thesis]].

## Valuation spread (the diversification case, off-extreme)

| Region | Fwd P/E | CAPE |
|---|---|---|
| US | ~21–22× | 34.7 |
| Europe / EAFE | ~15–17× | DE 24 / UK 20 |
| EM | ~13× | China 18 / India 34 |

EM-vs-DM discount ~35% = **~15-yr widest**; US-vs-ex-US wide but off-extreme (SSGA: near 25-yr avg).
The steelman bear case (US premium partly earned; "cheap ex-US" a 10-yr value trap; FX cuts both ways
for a EUR investor; EM dispersion) is carried in [[investment/theses/ex-us-de-dollarization#steelman-bear-case-carried-not-buried|the thesis]].
**Net: size for risk-reduction, not conviction outperformance.**
